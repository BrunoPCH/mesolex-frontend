Tlahuitoltepec Mixe

Tlahuitoltepec Mixe tlah1239 is part of a cluster of closely related, mutually intelligible varieties of Mixe collectively known as South Highland Mixe, spoken by approx. 17,000 people in five municipios located in the southwestern highlands of the Distrito Mixe in eastern Oaxaca. In Tlahuitoltepec the language is called Ayuujk.
